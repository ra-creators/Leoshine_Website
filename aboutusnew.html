About Us

"Life is Simple......Eat, Drink & Enjoy"

S.R. Group came into existence in 1987 since then we are involved in trading of Home Appliances. In 2008 the group started manufacturing under it's own brand named 'Leo Shine' it got started with 5 products namely Mixer Grinder, Gas Stove, Pressure Cooker, Hand Press Juicer and Lighter which has now extended to more than 200 products including Chimney and Induction.

The best part of this brand is the product quality which is fabulous and that too at much lesser price. Services are also available promptly.

Why Choose Us

From day one emphasis was laid on Quality and Innovation, the brand growing day by day. Inspite of Competition from all around "LEO SHINE" enjoys high brand, Institution and its Customers with every passing generation in India.
The Products manufactured by "LEO SHINE" can be matched with best products available in the world. Lot of Research and Development has taken place to understand needs of consumers. With our experience we have been able to keep up the elegance and durability intact.
The commitment in both the cases in terms of value addition to the product and its utility is always done with highest attention.
